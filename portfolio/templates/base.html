About

Hi, I'm Brenden

I'm a full-stack developer located in Washington state, with a wide background of frameworks and languages. Creativity has always been something that comes naturally to me, and when I graduated from high school in 2021, I didn't know what to do with my life.

I decided to take a gap year and work in retail to save my money. Along the way I bought my first car, received a promotion, and saved up as much money as I could for school. I heard about Code Fellows Bootcamp through one of my friends, and at first I was skeptical. Though I had always been interested in tech, so I decided to give it a try.

Immediately I clicked with code. I worked my way up from working with HTML and CSS to make basic static sites off of github, to creating full-stack web applications in a little over a year. Code is the perfect place I can show off my creativity and design skills, creating software that is easy to use and functional is one of my many passions.

When I'm not coding, I am probably gaming, listening to music, or skateboarding (when its nice out). Some of my favorite games include Resident Evil, Spider-man 2, MGS5, Sifu and Dying light. I'm also a huge fan of anime and comics, I have a big collection of Spider-man comics and various manga series like Chainsaw Man, One Punch Man, Blood Lad and Soul Eater.

Projects

Viper's Venom Tavern

Viper's Venom Tavern, the number one place to buy, sell and trade cards! VVT for short is a E-commerce website made card players looking to expand or sell their collection. User's create an account with a username, email and password. From there you can browse the store, put up your own cards for sale, and view the about page. VVT is still a work in progress, with new features being implemented and reworked.

Full stack web application made with Next.Js and Django Rest framework, styled using Tailwind css and deployed using Postgres.

Text Adventure

Text Adventure is a throw back to old school text games from the 70's. Take on the role of an adventurer searching for the mystical Idol of Power! In this game, user's must manage their health and stamina while traversing through the many environments to find the Idol. Solve puzzles, fight creatures, and make it out alive! Each play through will be unique, as each environment has different scenarios that could play out. Do you have what it takes to survive the dangerous jungle of Anradesha?

Command line text game programmed using Python and styled using rich.
